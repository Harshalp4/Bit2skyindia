# Bit2Sky — AI Image & Illustration Prompts

The site ships with **premium coded placeholder visuals** (gradient dashboards,
phone mockups, product frames) so it looks polished with zero image assets.
When you generate real images, drop them into `public/images/` and replace the
`<ProductVisual />` / `<DashboardMockup />` usages with `next/image`.

Recommended export: **1600×1000px (16:10), WebP/PNG, < 300KB**.
OG/social image: **1200×630px** → `public/images/og-cover.png`.

---

## Brand visual direction
Premium, modern, minimal, enterprise. Deep navy `#0B1B3D`, royal blue `#2563EB`,
sky blue `#38BDF8`, white + slate neutrals. Subtle gradients, soft shadows,
clean grids. Reference quality: Microsoft, Stripe, Vercel, Accenture.
